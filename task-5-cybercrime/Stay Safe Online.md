# 🛡️ Stay Safe Online: Prevention Checklist

As a Student Digital Ambassador, I recommend following these 8 actionable steps to stay secure:

1. **UPI PIN Security:** Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money; it is strictly for authorizing outbound payments.
2. **Verify Merchant Identity:** Always verify the name of the recipient in your UPI app before confirming any transaction.
3. **Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):** Use 2FA on your college email and all social media accounts to prevent unauthorized access.
4. **Avoid Public Wi-Fi for Banking:** Never perform financial transactions while connected to open or public Wi-Fi networks.
5. **Check for Phishing Links:** Do not click on suspicious links in SMS or WhatsApp claiming your bank account is "blocked."
6. **Use Strong, Unique Passwords:** Ensure your VITyarthi and college email passwords are complex and unique.
7. **Official App Downloads:** Only download banking or UPI apps from the official Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
8. **Screen Sharing Caution:** Never share your screen via apps like AnyDesk if someone claiming to be "support" asks you to do so.

---

### 🚨 How to Report Cybercrime
If you or a peer encounter a scam, report it immediately to the official Indian authorities:
* **National Cyber Crime Portal:** cybercrime.gov.in(https://cybercrime.gov.in)
* **National Helpline Number:** **1930** (Available 24x7)
